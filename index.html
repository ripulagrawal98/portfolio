Projects

  • Scheduling App for Individual with Autism

    Autism is a group of complex disorder which impairs the ability to communicate of children with ASD. As research shows that symptoms of ASD vary tremendously from individual to individual and also this disorder is not curable but its symptoms can be cured to reduce its effect and improve the performance of child.

    One such symptoms is their resistant to change in schedule either it is minor change or some major/sudden change as they feel irritate or do repetitive things, also they need special assistance in doing their work either from their caretakers or parents.

    To solve this issue I have designed an android app with the following features :

    • Scheduling App will provide them a fix set of schedule of daily tasks on particular date,feed by their caretakers/parents.
    • Visual Prompting will be there at the scheduled time corresponding with that activity. It will be either audio/video/set of images to assist the children in performing that activity

  • Sentiment Analysis for drugs/medicines

    The training dataset contains reviews for 102 different drugs along with their sentiments as positive, negative, neutral. The language used in this is not grammatically correct as some uses sarcasm and expresses their sentiments w.r.t. each drug. One thing is like one review can have different sentiment for different drugs respectively. So to make predictions for sentiments on unseen data I've used NLP based technique i.e. Bag of Words to make features and also applied the name of drugs respect to each review. As it has three classes to predict so it becomes multi-classification so for an algorithm I've used Support Vector Classifier. In the end, to measure how relevant how our model is we've measure F1 score.
